§ 05

Booking Checklist

Book now / very soon

  • Treviso flights + rental car Saturday turnover = peak price. Lock now.
  • Ortisei accommodation 3 nights (4–7 Jul). Fills fast for July.
  • Cortina accommodation 3 nights (8–10 Jul). Most expensive base.
  • Seceda cable car · 6 Jul seceda.it ↗ — bookable now
  • Col Raiser cable car · 7 Jul colraiser.it ↗
  • Lagazuoi cable car · 10 Jul lagazuoi.it ↗

Set calendar reminders

  • ~9 Jun 2026 · Tre Cime parking Rolling 1-month window opens. €40, 12-hour slot. auronzo.info ↗
  • Early Jun 2026 · Lago di Braies €44 combo (transit + parking + shuttle + food voucher). prags.bz ↗
  • Sunrise tip For Braies on 10 Jul, arrive before 9 AM and you may not need a booking — restriction is 9 AM – 4 PM.
